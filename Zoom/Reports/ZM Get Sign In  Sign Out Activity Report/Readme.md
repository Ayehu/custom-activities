<br>#     Zoom</br>
<br>Get Sign In / Sign Out Activity Report</br>

* Pro or higher plan.
**Scopes:** `report:read:admin`
 </br>

<br>OperationID: reportSignInSignOutActivities</br>
<br>EndPoint:</br>
<br>/report/activities</br>
