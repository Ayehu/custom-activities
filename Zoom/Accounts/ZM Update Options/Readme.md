#     Zoom


Update Options

Update a Sub Account's options under the Master Account. Your account must be a Master Account in order to update the options for Sub Accounts. Zoom only assigns this privilege to trusted partners. 

**Prerequisites:**
* Pro or a higher paid account with Master Account option enabled. 
**Scope**: `account:write:admin`
 

Method: Patch

OperationID: accountOptionsUpdate

EndPoint:

/accounts/{accountId}/options
